    Terms Of Service

    By commissioning me, you are agreeing to the terms of service below.
    In case of any non-compliance, the commission process can be cancelled.

    I. General

    1. Provide references/descriptions (Note: If not provided enough, I can use my own creativity).

    2. I will not accept requests that I don't feel comfortable to. Please consider this and my art style.

    3. Some things I don't usually do, but I'm willing to try: NSFW, Furry, Mecha (Cyberpunk-related), Twitch Badges and/or Emotes.

    II. Payment

    1. Payment must at least half before working on the commission (sketch), and the other half at the end (finished) or the full payment before.

    2. The price can change depending on the complexity.

    3. An invoice is sent to your Paypal once everything is agreed.

    4. Changes after the drawing is finished will be charged apart.

    5. For brazilian customers: I'll accept payments via PIX (20% discount)

    III. Process

    1. After everything is set up, The sketch will be made and sent to the customer. If satisfied, the work will be based on this sketch.

    2. The time of your order depends on queue (if any) and complexity. Estimated time is 5 days.

    3. You can check the commissions processes in my Trello!

    4. If any problem occurs, the customer will be notified as soon as possible!

    IV. Considerations

    1. I will do everything I can to make what you want! However, I won't change the finished product. So here's a little advice: Ask for some changes once you receive the sketch!

    2. "With great changes comes extra charge." - Uncle Bam. (Outfit changes, addition or subtraction, anything that makes me have to redraw it completely.

    3. Be clear in what you want!

    V. Copyright

    1. The customer can repost and use the commissioned piece, credits are very appreciated!

    2. The customer is allowed to use the copyrighted artwork for personal use only unless agreed otherwise.

    3. The customer CANNOT reproduce/use the copyrighted artwork commercially.

    4. The customer CANNOT take credits for the creation of the artwork.
